THE FIRST STUDIO ON A SAILBOAT

The idea of a nomadic design studio, we thought, was the best way to stay creative. Seeing new people and places, enriches our work. We created our two first games (Hiversaires and Oquonie) while in Tokyo and Thailand, and love how those experiences impacted our projects. It is something we want to continue to do, and being in a studio that moves will help us accomplish that.

BECOME A PATRON

Patreon help fund artists as they produce smaller works like music, art and tools.

To give a donation, go to our {{Patreon page|https://patreon.com/100}} and click on the Become a patron button. You have the option of choosing a reward (each tier has different rewards), and that's it!

Your donations permits us to continue to produce open-source content. We believe that tools should always be free, your donations will help ensure this. Our games, while being paid games, are open source too and viewable on our {{Github|https://github.com/hundredrabbits}}.

Your support also goes into creating our monthly videos that go in depth on our projects, and life on a sailboat (that is free for everyone to watch). We have learned a lot from the shared experiences of others, we hope that our videos will have the same effect.

All the money we get is re-invested back into the Hundredrabbits projects. With your support we can also pay for internet and for the inevitable - and incessant - upkeep of our devices (which often break at sea, and from continued use).

Thank you for your support!
Rekka & Devine
